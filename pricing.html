Private vs NHS Dental Fees in Belfast

Transparency matters. Compare the estimated costs of fast-track Private Care against standard NHS / HSC fees. Note that cosmetic treatments and advanced implants are strictly not available under the NHS.

💡 NHS (HSC NI) Rules: In Northern Ireland, NHS patients pay 80% of the treatment cost up to a maximum cap of £384 per course of treatment. Wait times for NHS appointments can be significantly longer than Private bookings.

Routine & Exam

  • New Patient Consultation
    Private: £65 - £100 NHS: ~£15 - £25
  • Routine Check-up
    Private: £60 - £70 NHS: ~£15 - £20
  • Hygiene (Scale & Polish)
    Private: £65 - £95 NHS: ~£15 - £30
  • Small X-Rays (per film)
    Private: £15 - £25 NHS: ~£5 - £15

Restorative Treatments

  • Fillings
    Private (White): £75 - £250 NHS (Silver): ~£20 - £60
  • Crowns
    Private (Porcelain): £750 - £989 NHS (Metal): ~£100 - £150
  • Root Canal Treatment
    Private: £400 - £766 NHS: ~£50 - £120
  • Tooth Extractions
    Private: £85 - £308 NHS: ~£20 - £60
  • Dentures (Acrylic)
    Private: £500 - £1,200 NHS: ~£100 - £300

Cosmetics & Orthodontics

  • Professional Teeth Whitening
    Private: £299 - £550 NHS: Not Available
  • Invisalign (Clear Aligners)
    Private: £2,500 - £5,500 NHS: Not Available
  • Porcelain Veneers (per tooth)
    Private: £750 - £989 NHS: Not Available
  • Composite Bonding
    Private: £200 - £350 NHS: Not Available

Implants & Surgery

  • Single Dental Implant
    Private: £2,078 - £2,500 NHS: Not Available*
  • Bone Grafting
    Private: £600 - £900 NHS: Not Available
  • CT Scan (CBCT)
    Private: £250 - £340 NHS: Not Available
  • Full Arch Implants (All-on-4)
    Private: From £10,000 NHS: Not Available

* Implants are only provided on the NHS in extremely rare medical circumstances (e.g., severe facial trauma or oral cancer reconstruction).
