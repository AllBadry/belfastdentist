Who We Are

Redefining Dental Care Access in Belfast.

The Belfast Dentist Network is a premier dental concierge platform designed to bridge the gap between patients seeking exceptional oral care and the top-rated clinics across the city.

Our Mission

Finding the right dentist shouldn't be a painful process. We built this platform with a single goal: to make discovering, comparing, and contacting high-quality dental practices as fast and seamless as possible.

Whether you are seeking the fastest available booking for a routine check-up, or the most efficient way to communicate with local clinics for cosmetic enhancements like Invisalign, our automated dispatch system ensures your request reaches the right professionals instantly.

Why Patients Trust Us

  • 100% Free Service: Our concierge matching service is completely free for patients. No hidden fees.
  • Speed & Efficiency: Our priority dispatch ensures clinics are notified the second you click submit.
  • Data Privacy: We route your request securely, ensuring your contact details are only shared with the clinics you choose.

How Our Smart System Works

We've eliminated the hassle of finding a dentist. Here is exactly what happens when you use our search engine.

1

Discover & Choose

Our engine scans Belfast based on your exact postcode, calculating precise distances to nearby clinics. You can either contact them manually using the provided details, or select multiple clinics to let us do the heavy lifting.

2

Secure Auto-Dispatch

If you choose auto-dispatch, we send a priority email to your selected clinics on your behalf. We protect your privacy—we only share your name and dental issue initially, keeping your contact details masked until a clinic responds.

3

You Are In Control

When clinics reply to our system, their response is immediately forwarded to your inbox. You are entirely in control—you decide which clinic to book with and politely decline the rest.

A Note to Participating Clinics: Our platform forwards patient requests simultaneously to multiple chosen providers. Patients are advised that they have the freedom to choose only one provider. Therefore, the clinic that responds the fastest significantly increases its chances of securing the patient's booking.
